<div class="notice notice-info">Reconstructed from Machiavelli corporate archives; dates massaged by a thousand generations of in-house historians. Treat as directional.</div>

- **c. 900u** — News of Union's founding reaches Gotia VII via a passing trade vessel. The news is largely ignored; Gotia's forge-clans consider themselves sovereign.
- **c. 1800u** — The **Great Vein Collapse** kills thousands in Dominus Arc-Smelting's flagship pit. A weakened Dominus is absorbed by Forge-House Machiavelli in the aftermath, consolidating Machiavelli's grip on the planet's heavy industry.
- **c. 3000u** — Forge-House Machiavelli formally rebrands as **The Machiavelli Corporation**, adopting Second Committee corporate structures while remaining entirely outside Union oversight.
- **c. 3900u** — **The Hollowing.** The last of Gotia VII's surface biosphere collapses. Remaining wild forests burn in uncontrolled slag-fires; bioluminescent flora vanish within a generation.
- **c. 4400u** — Machiavelli unilaterally issues the **Ash Accords**, claiming all planetary mineral rights. Remaining independent miner-clans are crushed or absorbed. CEO lineage consolidates into the Dominic family.
