**Status:** Humiliated. Watching. Reassessing.

The Armory's "peacekeeping detachment" was blindsided by the SSC kinetic strike. General Vargas's annexation play has been pre-empted by a rival corporation doing the annexing for him, without Union sanction, and the Armory was caught flat-footed in orbit. Vargas has not left his flagship in weeks. Armory warships still bristle above Gotia VII, but they have not fired a shot.

- **Priorities:** Publicly: demand Union arbitration. Privately: wait for SSC to overextend, then step in as the "stabilising force" Vargas always wanted to be. Sell ordnance to Machiavelli through plausibly-deniable intermediaries in the meantime.
- **Public face:** General Vargas, increasingly erratic, issuing statements demanding "Union clarity" that nobody at Central Committee seems willing to provide.
- **Weakness:** Every week SSC consolidates, the Armory's window closes. If Vargas waits too long he ends up garrisoning an SSC protectorate instead of running the planet himself.
