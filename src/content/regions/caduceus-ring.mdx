order: 7
---

Gotia VII's orbital belt is a crowded, contested layer of stations, freighters, and warships. **Caduceus Station** sits at its heart: originally Smith Shimano Corpro's orbital research facility, now the de facto capital of the occupation. **Port Aeturnus**, the planet's primary IPS-N groundside spaceport, is the Ring's anchor to the surface. Harrison Armory's warships hold a higher orbit, watching. The derelict IPSN freighter *Solitude*, site of the Lost Cargo job, still drifts in a decaying orbit on the far side of the planet, a reminder that the Ring has been a graveyard for a long time.
