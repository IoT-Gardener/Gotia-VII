## Registry Notes

R.R.R. analysts are still parsing the energy spikes and localised destruction aboard Caduceus Station the week Replica surfaced. Her own account is that she woke in a lab, hacked the local security network, turned the station's automated defences on anyone attempting to stop her escape, and slaved the pilot data of the first mech she came across - a Black Witch - before dropping suborbital to the Gotian surface. She has not offered to explain the gap between "woke up" and "hacked station security" in anything like a satisfying way.

### Provenance

Created in one of Smith-Shimano Corpro's classified gene-engineering labs, Replica is a prototype in a long line of attempts to facilitate human engram transference. Clone bodies, heavy neural implants, and - crucially - an Enlightenment-Class NHP appropriated from Shepard-Oaks research after the MONIST-1 incident on Eden Lambda. The stated goal of the programme is to skirt the First Contact Accords on the grounds that "the FCA is null and void with Ra's banishment" and to let SSC shareholders live forever. Hanako was intended to host one such shareholder. She declined.

### Subjectivity

Replica is, by her own admission, in the middle of an identity crisis. Two threads in particular:

1. Whether she is actually a human operator, or the NHP driving a meat puppet.
2. If she is human, how much of what she calls preference is genuinely hers versus residue from the woman she was cloned from.

She asks a lot of questions about what things are "supposed" to taste like.

### Capability Profile

- Technophile and Iconoclast talents from the outset. Carries her Enlightenment-Class NHP with her at all times; analyst consensus is that it lives inside her cybernetic implants rather than a separate chassis.
- Dataplating and Subjectivity Enhancement Suite. She interfaces neurologically with nearly any accessible system and can either hack it, turn it, or tear it apart from the inside.
- Lives in cyberspace. Markedly less comfortable in meatspace - expect awkward social calibration, occasional latency in conversation, over-reliance on spoofed biometrics.

### Engagement Terms

Replica has offered her stolen mech, her hacking skill, and - of material interest to the Registry - the biometric data of an unnamed SSC director, in exchange for Registry assistance avoiding SSC reclamation attempts. She is off-books. Her contract is handled via Dispatch direct; no ledger trace, no manna withdrawals through standard channels.
