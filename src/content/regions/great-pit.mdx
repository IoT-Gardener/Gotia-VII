A monstrous open-pit mine in Gotia VII's northern hemisphere, the largest single excavation on the planet. The Pit has been chewed into the crust for over seven thousand years and now descends more than three kilometres. The city of **The Precipice** clings to its cliff face. Beneath its floor, an abandoned network of service tunnels hides the Rust & Ruin Registry's underground command.
