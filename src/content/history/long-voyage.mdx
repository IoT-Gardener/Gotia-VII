- **c. 5007bu** — The 7th ship of The Ten, *Corliss*, departs from Earth (later known as Cradle), carrying cryo-banked settlers and the industrial seed-libraries of a dozen Fall-era forge guilds.
- **c. 4180bu** — *Corliss* makes planetfall on Gotia VII after an extended sublight transit. Surveyors catalogue the planet's ruinous atmosphere and its staggering mineral, isotopic, and geothermal wealth. The decision is made to stay.
- **c. 4100bu** — *Corliss* is partially stripped to build **Foundry-Prime**, the first permanent settlement. The ship's reactor becomes the planet's first geothermal tap.
- **c. 3700bu** — Deep-mine operations begin in earnest. Miner-families, bound to specific seams and shafts, form the social backbone of the young colony.
- **c. 2500bu** — The Soot Generations begin. Atmospheric scrubbers fail or are cannibalised for industry; the population adapts, biologically and culturally, to the permanent smog.
- **c. 500bu** — The Corliss Council, last unified governing body of the original settlers, dissolves. Power fragments between mining syndicates and forge-clans, chief among them **Forge-House Machiavelli** and its rival **Dominus Arc-Smelting**.
